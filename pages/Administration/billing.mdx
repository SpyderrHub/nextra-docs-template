# Account Administration

The **Administration** section is where you manage your workspace identity, subscription tiers, and credit usage. QuantisAI is designed to scale with your project, providing transparent tools to track your resource consumption.

## Workspace Overview

Your workspace is the central hub for all your AI generations. You can access these settings by clicking on your profile icon in the sidebar and selecting **Settings**.

### Account Profile
- **Workspace Name**: Customize your workspace name for organizational purposes.
- **Email Notifications**: Toggle alerts for credit usage milestones (e.g., when you reach 80% of your monthly character limit).
- **API Keys**: Generate and rotate secret keys for integrating QuantisAI into your applications.

## Subscription & Billing

QuantisAI uses a tiered subscription model combined with pay-as-you-go flexibility to ensure you never pay for more than you need.

### Managing Plans
You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time through the **Billing** tab:
- **Starter**: Essential features for new creators.
- **Creator**: Perfect for professionals requiring more characters and priority email support.
- **Pro**: The ultimate synthesis experience with high-volume character limits and daily Pro generations.

### Credit System
Credits are consumed based on the characters synthesized in the TTS Studio or the duration of audio processed in the STT and Music Generator tools.
- **Renewal**: Monthly character quotas reset on your billing anniversary date.
- **Top-ups**: If you exhaust your monthly quota, you can purchase one-time credit top-ups to keep your production moving without upgrading your entire plan.

## User Management

*Note: Team features are available on Creator and Pro plans.*

Collaborate with your team by inviting members to your workspace. QuantisAI allows you to share a central pool of credits while maintaining individual generation histories.

### Inviting Members
1. Navigate to **Administration > Team**.
2. Enter the email address of the team member.
3. Select their **Role**:
    - **Admin**: Full access to billing, API keys, and user management.
    - **Member**: Can use the studio and API but cannot change billing or invite others.

### Security & Authentication
QuantisAI supports modern authentication methods to keep your workspace secure:
- **Social Auth**: Quick login via Google or GitHub.
- **Token Verification**: Integrated token logic for all API requests to ensure unauthorized users cannot access your credits.

## Usage Analytics

Monitor your workspace activity through the **Usage Dashboard**. 
- **Real-time Tracking**: View a breakdown of character usage across different tools (TTS vs. STT).
- **Cost Transparency**: Compare your current usage against traditional providers to see your "96% Savings" in real-time.
- **Generation History**: A full audit log of all files generated within the workspace, including timestamps and the user who initiated the request.
