# Getting Started with QuantisAI

To begin generating studio-quality AI audio, you first need to set up your QuantisAI account. This guide will walk you through the signup process, logging in, and configuring your workspace for the first time.

---

## Creating an Account

Setting up an account is free and grants you access to our basic synthesis tools and a monthly allowance of free characters.

1.  **Visit the Signup Page**: Navigate to [quantisai.org/signup](https://www.quantisai.org/signup).
2.  **Choose Your Method**:
    * **Social Signup**: Use your **Google** or **GitHub** account for a one-click registration.
    * **Email Signup**: Enter your preferred email address and create a strong password.
3.  **Verification**: If you signed up via email, check your inbox for a verification link. Clicking this link activates your account and ensures you receive important billing and usage alerts.

## Logging In

Once your account is active, you can access the QuantisAI Studio from any device.

* **Standard Login**: Enter your credentials at the [Login Page](https://www.quantisai.org/login).
* **Persistent Sessions**: Select "Remember Me" to stay logged in on your personal device for quicker access to the dashboard.
* **Password Recovery**: If you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password" link to receive a reset token via email.

---

## Initial Setup

After your first login, the platform will guide you through a brief initialization to tailor the experience to your needs.

### 1. Workspace Identity
You will be prompted to name your **Workspace**. This is especially important for **Creator** and **Pro** users who plan to invite team members later. Your workspace name will appear on all shared projects and billing invoices.

### 2. Selecting Your Primary Use Case
QuantisAI will ask for your primary area of interest to optimize your dashboard:
* **Content Creation**: Prioritizes long-form TTS and video-ready voices.
* **Development & API**: Highlights API documentation and token management.
* **Enterprise/Batch**: Focuses on high-volume processing and speed.

### 3. API Key Generation
For developers, your first API key is generated during this setup. You can view, copy, or rotate this key in the **Settings > API** section. Keep this key secure, as it grants access to your character quota.

### 4. Claiming Your Free Credits
Every new account starts with **3,000 free characters** (watermarked). These are automatically credited to your account so you can test our premium Hindi and English voices immediately in the [Text to Speech Studio](/docs/text-to-speech).

## Account Security

We recommend the following steps to ensure your generations and data remain secure:
* **Rotate API Keys**: Regularly rotate your API keys if they are used in public-facing applications.
* **Monitor Usage**: Check the **Credits Dashboard** regularly to identify any unexpected spikes in usage.
