# AI Music Generator

The **QuantisAI Music Generator** is a powerful, Pro-tier tool designed for creators who need custom, high-quality background music, soundtracks, or loops without the complexity of traditional production.

## Feature Overview

Our music generation engine allows you to transform text-based prompts and style settings into full-blown musical compositions with ultra-low latency.

- **Pro-Tier Access**: This feature is available to users on the **Pro** plan, offering the highest throughput and most advanced synthesis models.
- **High-Fidelity Output**: Generates studio-quality audio suitable for professional video production, gaming, and streaming.
- **Seamless Dashboard Integration**: Easily navigate between your music projects and other QuantisAI tools like TTS and Voice Cloning.

## The Generation Workflow

### 1. Style & Prompting
To begin, describe the vibe, genre, or mood you want to achieve.
- **Descriptive Prompts**: Use detailed prompts to guide the AI (e.g., "Lo-fi hip hop beat with a chill, rainy atmosphere").
- **Style Presets**: Choose from curated styles to quickly set the foundation for your track.

### 2. Composition Settings
Fine-tune the technical aspects of your generation:
- **Track Duration**: Select the length of the track to fit your specific needs.
- **Complexity**: Adjust the layering and instrument density of the output.

### 3. Generation and Preview
Once configured, the engine processes your request globally on our distributed edge infrastructure.
- **Processing**: Tracks typically generate in seconds, allowing for rapid iteration.
- **Live Preview**: Listen to your generated track directly in the browser before using your credits to finalize.

## Asset Management

### Music Library
All your generated tracks are stored in your central repository for easy access.
- **Organization**: Filter and sort your tracks by date, style, or project name.
- **Downloads**: Export your finalized tracks in high-quality formats for use in external editing software.

## Best Practices
- **Be Specific**: The more descriptive your text prompt, the more accurate the instrumentation and mood will be.
- **Iterative Design**: Use short previews to test different styles before committing to long-form generations.
- **Check Credits**: Monitor your **Credits Dashboard** to track your daily Pro generation usage.
