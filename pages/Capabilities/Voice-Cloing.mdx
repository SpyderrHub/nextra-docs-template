# AI Voice Cloning

The **QuantisAI Voice Cloning** tool allows you to create an exact digital replica of a human voice. Once cloned, these "Voice Twins" can be used across the platform to generate speech from text, providing unparalleled consistency for brand identity, content creation, and personalized messaging.

## How Voice Cloning Works

Our engine uses advanced neural networks to analyze the unique timbre, pitch, and prosody of a target voice. Unlike traditional synthesis, cloning captures the "soul" of the voice, including characteristic breaths and emotional nuances.

### Requirements for a Perfect Clone
To achieve studio-quality results, your training data should meet the following criteria:
- **Audio Length**: Minimum 1 minute of clear speech (3–5 minutes recommended for best results).
- **Environment**: Clean audio with zero background noise, music, or echoes.
- **Content**: Natural, conversational speech without extreme shouting or whispering.
- **Format**: Supports high-quality WAV, MP3, or M4A uploads.

## The Cloning Process

### 1. Uploading Samples
Navigate to the **Voice Cloning** section of your dashboard.
- **Direct Upload**: Drag and drop your high-quality audio samples.
- **Recording**: You can also record directly through your browser if you have a professional-grade microphone setup.

### 2. Labeling and Metadata
Give your voice a descriptive name (e.g., "Narrator - Professional Male") and assign tags such as "Calm," "Energetic," or "Hindi-Accent" to help organize your library.

### 3. Training
Click **Create Voice**. Our distributed edge infrastructure will begin processing the data. 
- **Processing Time**: Cloning typically takes between 30 seconds and 2 minutes depending on the length of the sample.
- **Notification**: You will see a "Ready" status in your voice library once the model is finalized.

## Using Your Cloned Voice

Once the cloning process is complete, your custom voice is immediately available in the **Text to Speech Studio**.
- **Selection**: Go to the TTS Studio and look for the "Cloned" or "My Voices" tab in the voice library.
- **Consistency**: The cloned voice will maintain its unique characteristics across any text you provide, regardless of the script's length.

## Security and Ethical Usage

At QuantisAI, we prioritize the ethical use of AI technology.
- **Ownership**: You must own the rights or have explicit permission to clone a voice.
- **Verification**: For high-profile or enterprise cloning, we may require additional verification steps to prevent unauthorized deepfakes.
- **Privacy**: Your training data and cloned models are encrypted and stored securely; they are never shared with other users or used to train public models.
