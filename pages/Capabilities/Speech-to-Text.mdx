# Speech to Text

The **QuantisAI Speech to Text** tool leverages state-of-the-art automatic speech recognition (ASR) models to provide lightning-fast, highly accurate transcriptions. Designed for researchers, content creators, and developers, it handles everything from short voice notes to long-form video content.

## Key Features

- **Multilingual Recognition**: Support for over 50+ languages, including specialized optimization for Hindi and regional dialects.
- **Ultra-Low Latency**: Powered by our distributed edge infrastructure, ensuring your transcriptions are ready in a fraction of the playback time.
- **Speaker Diarization**: (Available in Pro) Automatically identifies and labels different speakers within a single audio file.
- **Punctuation & Formatting**: Intelligent neural processing that adds natural punctuation, capitalization, and paragraph breaks.

## Getting Started

### 1. Uploading Media
Navigate to the **Speech to Text** tab in your dashboard. You can provide source material in two ways:
- **File Upload**: Drag and drop your audio or video files (MP3, WAV, MP4, etc.) directly into the upload zone.
- **URL Import**: Paste a link to a hosted file or supported video platform to fetch the audio automatically.

### 2. Configuration
Before starting the transcription, customize the output settings:
- **Source Language**: Select the language spoken in the audio. You can also choose "Auto-Detect" to let the AI identify the language for you.
- **Task Type**: Choose between **Transcription** (text in the original language) or **Translation** (converting the speech directly into English text).
- **Timestamping**: Enable timestamps if you need to align the text with specific moments in the video or audio file.

### 3. Processing
Once you click **Transcribe**, the system begins processing. You can monitor the progress bar in the workspace. For large files, you can navigate away from the page; the task will continue in the background and appear in your history once complete.

## Managing Transcripts

### The Editor Workspace
After processing, your text appears in an interactive editor where you can:
- **Search & Replace**: Quickly fix specific terms or names.
- **Playback Sync**: Click on any word to play the audio from that exact timestamp, making verification effortless.
- **Export Options**: Download your final transcript in multiple formats, including:
    - **Plain Text (.txt)** for documentation.
    - **Subtitles (.srt or .vtt)** for video production.
    - **JSON** for developer integration.

## API Integration

For developers looking to automate transcription workflows, the `/v1/stt` endpoint supports multipart/form-data uploads for seamless integration into your own apps.

```bash
curl -X POST [https://api.quantisai.com/v1/stt](https://api.quantisai.com/v1/stt) \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -F "file=@audio.mp3" \
  -F "language=hi"
