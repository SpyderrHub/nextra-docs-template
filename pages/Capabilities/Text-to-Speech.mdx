# AI Text to Speech Studio

The **AI Text to Speech** studio is the core of the QuantisAI platform, enabling you to transform text into expressive, high-fidelity audio using advanced neural models.

## Interface Overview

The Studio is designed with a "developer-first" mentality, providing a streamlined workflow from text input to audio generation.

- **Sidebar Navigation**: Quickly switch between the Dashboard Overview, Generation History, and specialized AI tools like Voice Cloning and Music Generation.
- **Studio Header**: Displays your current tool (AI Text to Speech) and provides quick access to your account settings and credit balance.
- **Generation Workspace**: The central area where you input text and configure your voice settings.

## Getting Started: Generating Speech

### 1. Voice Selection
QuantisAI offers a diverse library of premium voices tailored for various use cases, including anime, cartoons, and professional advertisements.
- **Voice Library**: Access the full library to filter voices by gender, language (with a strong focus on Hindi), and style.
- **Voice Designer**: Use the **AI Voice Designer** to create unique, custom voices that fit your brand's specific personality (Available in Pro).

### 2. Inputting Text
Enter your script into the main text area. 
- **Character Limits**: Be mindful of your plan's character limits (e.g., 50,000 for Starter, 1,000,000 for Pro).
- **Pro Tip**: Use simple, modern language for the most natural-sounding results from the neural engine.

### 3. Advanced Settings
Fine-tune your audio output using the following parameters:
- **Audio Format**: Choose between common formats like MP3 for web use or high-fidelity 48kHz Stereo for professional production.
- **Latency Optimization**: The studio defaults to ultra-low latency (~2s) for fast previews and high-throughput batching.

## Managing Your Content

### Generation History
Every audio file you generate is automatically saved to your **History**.
- **Review & Download**: Replay previous generations or download them directly to your device.
- **Batch Management**: View the status of large-scale batch processing jobs.

### Credit Management
The **Credits** section provides a real-time view of your usage.
- **Quota Tracking**: Monitor how many characters you have remaining in your current billing cycle.
- **Upgrading**: If you need more capacity, you can upgrade to a Creator or Pro plan directly from the Dashboard.
