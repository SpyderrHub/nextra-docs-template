# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 23, 2026**

At QuantisAI, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of the data you entrust to us. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our website and AI synthesis services.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better experience for all our users.

### A. Personal Information
- **Account Details**: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and authentication data (via Google, GitHub, or email).
- **Billing Information**: For paid subscriptions, we collect payment details through our secure third-party processors. We do not store your full credit card numbers on our servers.

### B. Audio and Text Data
- **Input Text**: We store the text you submit for synthesis to provide you with a generation history.
- **Voice Samples**: Audio uploaded for **Voice Cloning** is stored in an encrypted format. This data is used solely to train your private voice model.
- **Generated Assets**: We store the audio files you generate (TTS, Music, etc.) so you can access and download them from your dashboard.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:
- **Service Delivery**: To process your requests, manage your account, and provide customer support.
- **Model Training (Private Only)**: Voice samples provided for cloning are used only to create your specific voice model. **We do not use your private voice samples or generated audio to train our public, foundational models.**
- **Platform Improvement**: We analyze anonymized usage patterns to optimize our infrastructure and reduce latency.

## 3. Data Retention and Security

### A. Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security protocols to protect your data:
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest.
- **Access Control**: API keys and tokens are required for all synthesis requests, ensuring only you can access your character quota and history.

### B. Retention
- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data as long as your account is active.
- **Deletion**: You can request the deletion of specific audio files or your entire account at any time. Once an account is deleted, all associated voice clones and history are purged from our active databases.

## 4. Sharing of Information

QuantisAI does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share information with:
- **Service Providers**: Third-party vendors who assist with billing (e.g., Stripe), hosting, and security.
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding your data:
- **Access and Portability**: You can download your generated audio and view your usage logs at any time.
- **Correction**: You can update your profile information through the **Account Settings**.
- **Opt-Out**: You can unsubscribe from marketing communications while still receiving essential service-related alerts.

## 6. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and remember your preferences. We do not use intrusive tracking cookies for third-party advertising.

## 7. Children's Privacy

QuantisAI is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
