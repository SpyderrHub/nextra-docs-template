## Best Practices

For the highest quality speech:

- Use proper punctuation.
- Write complete sentences.
- Use the language's native script whenever possible.
- Avoid mixing multiple languages in a single sentence unless intentional.
- Check spelling before generating audio.
- Keep abbreviations clear or spell them out if pronunciation is important.

---

## Mixed-Language Text

QuantisAI Labs can often generate speech from text containing multiple languages, such as English mixed with Hindi or other languages.

Example:

```text
Hello everyone, आज हम एक नया AI प्रोजेक्ट शुरू करेंगे।
```

Results may vary depending on the language combination and writing style.

---

## Language Quality

Speech quality can differ slightly between languages because of differences in pronunciation, writing systems, and available linguistic data.

In general, you can expect:

- Natural pronunciation
- Clear articulation
- Smooth pacing
- Human-like intonation
- High-quality audio output

The platform is continuously improved to provide better quality across all supported languages.

---

## Unsupported Characters

If your text contains unsupported symbols or uncommon characters, they may be ignored or pronounced unexpectedly.

Examples include:

- Random Unicode symbols
- Corrupted text
- Unsupported emoji sequences
- Invalid formatting characters
