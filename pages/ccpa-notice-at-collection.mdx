# CCPA Notice at Collection

**Effective Date: April 23, 2026**

This Notice at Collection is provided to California residents pursuant to the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). It describes the categories of personal information QuantisAI collects, the purposes for which we use that information, and your rights regarding your data.

---

## 1. Categories of Personal Information Collected

We collect information that identifies, relates to, or could reasonably be linked with you. In the past 12 months, QuantisAI has collected the following categories of personal information:

* **Identifiers**: Name, alias, postal address, unique personal identifier, online identifier, Internet Protocol (IP) address, email address, and account name.
* **Commercial Information**: Records of products or services purchased, obtained, or considered, and other purchasing or consuming histories or tendencies.
* **Biometric Information**: If you use our **Voice Cloning** service, we process audio recordings of your voice to create a unique vocal "digital twin." 
    * *Note: QuantisAI uses this data strictly for synthesis and does not use it for individual identification in a security or surveillance context.*
* **Internet or Other Electronic Network Activity**: Browsing history, search history, and information regarding your interaction with our website and API.
* **Geolocation Data**: Rough geographic location based on IP address to optimize latency via our edge infrastructure.
* **Professional or Employment-related Information**: If you apply for a job or provide business details for an Enterprise account.

## 2. Purposes for Collection and Use

QuantisAI uses the categories of personal information listed above for the following business and commercial purposes:

* **Providing Services**: To maintain your account, process payments, and generate AI audio (TTS, Music, and Voice Clones).
* **Safety and Security**: To detect security incidents, protect against malicious, deceptive, or illegal activity, and verify your identity.
* **Platform Optimization**: To debug and repair errors that impair existing intended functionality.
* **Customer Support**: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance.
* **Legal Compliance**: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

## 3. "Sale" or "Sharing" of Personal Information

QuantisAI **does not sell** your personal information for monetary value. However, like many online platforms, we may "share" identifiers and network activity with third-party service providers (such as analytics or cloud hosting partners) to improve our service performance.

* **No Training on Private Data**: We do not sell or share your specific voice samples or cloned models with third parties for their own use.

## 4. Retention Period

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this notice, or as required by law.
* **Account Data**: Retained for the duration of your active subscription.
* **Voice Samples**: Raw audio uploaded for cloning is stored until the model is generated or until you request its deletion.
* **Purge Policy**: Upon account deletion, all identifiable data is removed from our active production databases within 30 days.

## 5. Your Rights Under the CCPA

As a California resident, you have the following rights:
1.  **Right to Know**: You can request a disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
2.  **Right to Delete**: You can request that we delete the personal information we have collected from you.
3.  **Right to Correct**: You can request that we correct inaccurate personal information.
4.  **Right to Opt-Out of Sharing**: You can request that we stop sharing your information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising.
5.  **Right to Non-Discrimination**: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

## 6. How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise your rights, please submit a request via:
* **Email**: [privacy@quantisai.org](mailto:privacy@quantisai.org)
* **Web Form**: [Request Data Access/Deletion](https://www.quantisai.org/privacy-requests)

We will verify your request by asking you to provide information that matches what we have in our records (usually your account email and a verification code).
