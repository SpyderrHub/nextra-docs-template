# Quick Start

Welcome to QuantisAI Labs!

This guide will help you get up and running in just a few minutes. By the end of this guide, you'll know how to create an account, access the dashboard, and generate your first AI project.

---

## Before You Begin

Before using QuantisAI Labs, make sure you have:

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari)
- A stable internet connection
- A QuantisAI Labs account
- Available credits (if required for your selected feature)

---

# Step 1 — Create an Account

If you don't already have an account:

1. Open the QuantisAI Labs website.
2. Click **Sign Up**.
3. Continue with your preferred sign-in method.
4. Complete the registration process.

Once your account has been created, you'll be redirected to your dashboard.

> **Tip**
>
> Keep your account information secure and never share your login credentials.

---

# Step 2 — Explore the Dashboard

After signing in, you'll see the main dashboard.

From here you can access:

- Text to Speech
- Speech to Text
- Voice Design
- Credit Balance
- Generation History
- Account Settings
- API Access (if available)

Take a moment to explore the interface before creating your first project.

---

# Step 3 — Choose a Feature

Select the AI tool you want to use.

### Text to Speech

Convert written text into natural speech.

Best for:

- Videos
- Podcasts
- Voiceovers
- Audiobooks
- Presentations

---

### Speech to Text

Convert spoken audio into written text.

Best for:

- Meetings
- Interviews
- Video subtitles
- Podcasts
- Research

---

### Voice Design

Create unique AI voices using descriptive voice prompts.

Best for:

- Custom narrators
- Characters
- Brand voices
- Creative projects

---

# Step 4 — Create Your First Project

Each tool follows a simple workflow.

## Text to Speech

1. Open **Text to Speech**.
2. Select a voice.
3. Enter your text.
4. Adjust voice settings if needed.
5. Click **Generate**.
6. Preview the audio.
7. Download the generated file.

---

## Speech to Text

1. Open **Speech to Text**.
2. Upload your audio file.
3. Choose the transcription language if required.
4. Click **Transcribe**.
5. Review the generated transcript.
6. Copy or download the text.

---

## Voice Design

1. Open **Voice Design**.
2. Describe the voice you want to create.
3. Review your voice description.
4. Generate the voice.
5. Use the generated voice in Text to Speech.

---

# Step 5 — Download Your Results

After generation is complete, you can:

- Preview the output.
- Download the generated file.
- Copy the generated text (Speech to Text).
- Continue editing your project if supported.

---

# Credits

Some features consume credits when generating content.

Your available balance can be viewed from your dashboard.

Before starting large projects, make sure you have enough credits available.

For more information, visit the **Credits & Billing** section.

---

# Best Practices

For the best experience:

- Use correctly written text.
- Add punctuation for natural pauses.
- Upload clear, high-quality audio for transcription.
- Review your content before generating.
- Preview the results before downloading.
- Break very large projects into smaller sections when possible.

---

# Common Questions

### How long does generation take?

Most requests complete within a few seconds, although larger requests may take longer depending on the selected feature and server load.

---

### Can I use multiple languages?

Yes. QuantisAI Labs supports **600+ languages and regional dialects** across supported features.

---

### Can I download my results?

Yes. Once processing is complete, you can download your generated output directly from the platform.

---

### Can I use the platform without technical knowledge?

Absolutely. QuantisAI Labs is designed for both beginners and experienced developers.
